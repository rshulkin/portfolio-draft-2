Running can be one of the most challenging and rewarding physical activities- I know this first hand! If you're trying to get started, this is the place for you. Here, you'll find some beginner's tips and how to get started on your running journey. If you're here, that's already good news, because you've shown interest- you've begun! 70% of the battle is getting yourself to the starting line, so don't undermine the work it takes you to get to your first run. And your second. And your third run.
